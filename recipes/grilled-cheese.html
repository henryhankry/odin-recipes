Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A classic, yummy, simple treat for everyone in the family. Try it with any blend of cheeses you desire. Yum.

Ingredients

  • Cheese (any kind you like! Cheddar works great for your first try)
  • Whole wheat or white sandwich bread
  • butter or oil

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan on medium-high heat and melt butter or heat oil
  2. Put 2-3 slices of cheese between slices of bread
  3. Add sandwich to pan, and cook each side for 3-4 minutes, or until cheese is melted
