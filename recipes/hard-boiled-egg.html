Perfect Hard Boiled Eggs!

Eggs eggs eggs!

This method works every time! Use hard boiled eggs to add a little protein to your afternoon snack.

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Cold water
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  1. Place eggs in a pot and cover with cold water. Bring to boil, cover pot, and turn off heat. Let eggs cook in hot water for 9-12 minutes
  2. Immediately transfer eggs a bowl of ice water and chill for 5+ minutes.
  3. Peel by cracking the shell with a spoon and sliding the spoon along the contour of the egg.
